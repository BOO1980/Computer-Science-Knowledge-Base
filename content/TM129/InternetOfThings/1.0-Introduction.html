Introduction

1.0.1 Welcome to Create Your Own Smart Home Network

  • The Internet of Things (IoT) is vast. Sensors, actuators, and “smart” devices work together to collect and share data such as temperature, humidity, water levels, power, speed and so much more.
  • In the Exploring IoT with Packet Tracer course, you take a simple home network and turn it into a smart home network.

1.0.2 First Time in the Course

  • In this course, you will add, connect and control devices in a smart home network.
  • You will also learn to modify the script of an existing IoT device to create a new device.
  • The free Cisco Packet Tracer software lets you simulate networks without having access to physical equipment.

1.0.3 Why Should I Take This Course?

  • Many home security systems are powered by the Internet of Things (IoT).
  • There are many more IoT devices that you can add to make your home a Smart Home.
  • Packet Tracer contains many features to support the IoT. Packet Tracer has a wide variety of sensors, actuators, and smart devices that will allow you to design smart homes, smart cities, smart factories, and smart power grids.
  • You will learn to configure and modify scripts to make them function, and to control devices remotely.
