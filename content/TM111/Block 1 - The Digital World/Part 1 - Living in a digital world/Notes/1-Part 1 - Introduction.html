Introducing Part 1

  • You will learn about the historical development of communication technologies and computers and their role in today's society
  • You will consider examples of digital technologies in the world around you and their influence on your life
  • You will need to know that a digital technology is any technolgoy based on representing data as sequences of numbers
  • In this module, the term dgital world refers to the influence of computer on the world, in a wide variety of forms, linked by communications networks
  • Most of the use of technology that you'll consider will be legal and harmless
  • However you will also look at some examples that subvert laws and the rights of others
  • Some examples will move into the worlds of crime and terror, where information is transmitted that others want to intercept, control or suppress
  • Effective participation in the digital world therefore requires you to become aware of many technical, social and ethical pitfalls, as well as some ways you can avoid them
