1.2.1 - My Digital Identity

Introduction

  • Do you use social media to connect with others, to share posts, photos, and videos, or to stay informed about current events?
  • Have you considered how you posts project a particular image of yourself when you communicate with others

Whos reading my posts?

  • Friends and family
  • Colleagues and professional contacts
  • Followers and fans
  • Potential employers or clients

Summary

  • Your digital identity is shaped by your online interactions and the content you share.
  • Be mindful of who can see your posts and how they may interpret them.
  • Regularly review your privacy settings on social media platforms.
