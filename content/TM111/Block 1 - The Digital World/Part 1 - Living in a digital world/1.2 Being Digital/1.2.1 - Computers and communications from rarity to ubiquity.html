1.1 - Computers and communications: from rarity to ubiquity

Introduction

  • The theme of this section is the development of digital technologies, from the physically large and highly expensive equipment of the 1950s to the omnipresent networks and computer-based devices upon which our digital lives are founded today. In this section I will:
  • compare the development of the computer to the development of the telephone
  • describe some of the digital technologies that form an integral part of many of our lives
  • introduce the term information society.
