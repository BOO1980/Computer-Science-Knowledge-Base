MU123 Keywords

Unit 1:

  • BIDMAS: Brackets, Indices, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction
  • Powers: Another term for exponents, indicating the number of times a base is multiplied by itself.
  • Exponents: A mathematical notation indicating the number of times a quantity is multiplied by itself.
  • Sum: The result of adding two or more numbers.
  • Difference: The result of subtracting one number from another.
  • Product: The result of multiplying two or more numbers.
  • Quotient: The result of dividing one number by another.
