Technology Guide

Dataplotter and Graphplotter

  • Both resources are available from the MU123 website, within the Activities and Examples link
  • If you wish to use these resources outside of an activity, they can be accessed under the resources tab in the computing resources subpage
  • They can be also downloaded for offline use on your computer
  • Dataplotter and Graphplotter are presented via your web browser

Dataplotter

  • Dataplotter is used to generate various types of statistical data plots and to perform several statistical calculations
  • Dataplotter is able to plot up to two different datasets simultaneously, these are entered and displayed in the two columns
  • You can either enter your own data or use predefined datasets that correspond to different activities in your study units
  • Online help on the use of Dataplotter is available by clicking help at the top right hand corner of the application
