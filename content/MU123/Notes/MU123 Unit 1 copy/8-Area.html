Area

  • Area is the amount of space inside a 2D shape, measured in square units.
  • The area of a rectangle is calculated by multiplying its length by its width: Area = length × width.
  • The area of a triangle is calculated using the formula: Area = 0.5 × base × height.
  • The area of a circle is calculated using the formula: Area = π × radius², where π (pi) is approximately 3.14159.
  • To find the area of irregular shapes, you can divide them into regular shapes, calculate the area of each, and then sum them up.
