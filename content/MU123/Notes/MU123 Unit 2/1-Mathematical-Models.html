    • Suppose that a car travels a distance of 75 km in 45 minutes on a motorway. What is its average speed? Give your answer in km/h.
      • First, convert 45 minutes to hours: 45 minutes = 45/60 hours = 0.75 hours.
      • Now, use the formula for average speed: Average speed = Total distance / Total time.
      • Average speed = 75 km / 0.75 hours = 100 km/h.
