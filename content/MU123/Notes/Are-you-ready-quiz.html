Question 8: store is offering 15% off all electrical goods during its summer sale. What would you pay for a washing machine that usually costs £310?

To find the sale price, first calculate 15% of £310:

15% of £310 = 0.15 × £310 = £46.50

Now, subtract this discount from the original price:

Sale price = £310 - £46.50 = £263.50

Question 9: A family on holiday in Florida is considering buying a souvenir which is on sale for $22. If the rate of exchange at the time is £1 = $1.42 approximately how much does the souvenir cost in pounds?

To find the cost in pounds, divide the price in dollars by the exchange rate:

Cost in pounds = $22 ÷ $1.42 ≈ £15.49

Question 10: A garden is 7m x 5m and has a circular flower bed in the center. with a radius of 1.5m. . If 400 grams of grass seed covers 8 square metres, how much grass seed will be needed to re-seed the lawn?

First, calculate the area of the garden:

Area of garden = length × width = 7m × 5m = 35m²

Next, calculate the area of the circular flower bed using the formula A = πr²:

Area of flower bed = π × (1.5m)² = π × 2.25m² ≈ 7.07m²

Now, subtract the area of the flower bed from the area of the garden to find the area of the lawn:

Area of lawn = Area of garden - Area of flower bed = 35m² - 7.07m² ≈ 27.93m²

Finally, calculate the amount of grass seed needed:

Grass seed needed = (Area of lawn / Area covered by 400 grams) × 400 grams = (27.93m² / 8m²) × 400 grams ≈ 1396.5 grams

So, approximately 1396.5 grams of grass seed will be needed to re-seed the lawn.

Question 11: A plan from a DIY booklet shows a shelf and its metal bracket. The shelf makes a right angle with the wall. The size of one of the angles is given (28°). What is the size of the other angle?

Answer: Since the shelf makes a right angle with the wall, the two angles must add up to 90°. Therefore, the size of the other angle can be calculated as follows:
Other angle = 90° - 28° = 62°
So, the size of the other angle is 62°.
