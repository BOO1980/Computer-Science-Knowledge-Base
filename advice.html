Study Tips

Make progress visible

  • At the end of each session, add 1–2 bullet notes to the top of the relevant page.
  • Use consistent filenames like Unit-Topic.html so they sort nicely.

Spaced repetition

  • Review key concepts after 1 day, 7 days, and 30 days. Add a small “Review” note block to pages you want to revisit.

Practice first

  • Before reading solutions, write a 3–5 step plan for how you’d attempt a problem.

Pair study rhythm

  • Alternate “driver” and “navigator” roles every 25 minutes. The driver types; the navigator checks understanding and notes next steps.
